Roasted Pepper Frittata

Williams-Sonoma Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 3 unit(s) red bell pepper(s)
  • 2 clove(s) garlic
  • 2 teaspoon(s) balsamic vinegar
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) oregano dried
  • 8 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 3 tablespoon(s) milk
  • 0.5 cup(s) parmeggiano grated
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine bell pepper strips, garlic, vinegar, oregano and salt and pepper to taste. Let marinate 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, whisk eggs, milk and Parmesan until frothy. Add pepper mixture and mix well. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  4. Grease a banana bread Pyrex. Add egg mixture. Bake until center is cooked through.&nbsp;
  5. Alternatively, in a 10-inch nonstick ovenproof frying pan over medium-high heat, warm the olive oil. When oil is hot, add egg mixture and, when it starts to set, lift the edges of the frittata with a spatula so that some of the egg mixture runs underneath. Reduce heat to medium and cook until the bottom is set but the top is still runny, 8-10 minutes. Place pan in the oven and cook until eggs are set on top and golden brown on bottom, 6-7 minutes.
  6. <ol class="RecipeDirections-List"><li class="RecipeDirections-Step" data-step-id="6770794041d119070e7d8564">Remove frittata from oven and loosen with a spatula.&nbsp; Invert onto a serving plate and cut into wedges.</li></ol>
