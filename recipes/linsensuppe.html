Linsensuppe

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 0.25 pound(s) bacon cut into 1-inch strips
  • 2 unit(s) onion(s) sliced
  • 16 ounce(s) lentil(s)
  • 1 cup(s) celery chopped
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) thyme
  • 2 unit(s) bay leaf
  • 1 unit(s) potato grated
  • 2 quart(s) broth
  • 2 tablespoon(s) lemon juice
  • 2 unit(s) jalapeño pepper(s) seeded and quartered

Directions

  1. In large dutch oven, fry bacon until golden brown. Add celery, onions, carrots; saute until onions are tender.
  2. Add lentils, pepper, thyme, bay leaves, potato, jalapeno and broth to vegetables. Bring to a boil. Reduce and simmer, covered, 25 minutes.
  3. Remove bay leaves and jalapeño. Stir in lemon juice.
