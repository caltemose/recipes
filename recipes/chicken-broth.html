Chicken Broth

Ingredients

  • 1 unit(s) chicken cleaned
  • 2 stalk(s) celery broken into 3 pieces
  • 2 unit(s) carrot(s) cut in half
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) cut in quarters
  • 1 pinch peppercorn(s) to taste
  • 4 quart(s) water cold

Directions

    Notes

    Put the chicken in a very large pot. Add the celery, carrots, and onion. Cook on medium heat for a little while to lightly brown the ingredients. Add water, peppercorns and some salt. Cover the pot and simmer for up to 3 hours. If you are only going to cook for an hour or so, bring the water to a boil quickly and then reduce the heat to simmer. To remove the fat let the stock cool then refrigerate for a few hours to solidify the fat. It can then be removed easily.
