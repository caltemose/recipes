Stewed Fruit

Ingredients

  • 1 pound(s) prune(s) pitted
  • 10 ounce(s) apricots dried
  • 2 pound(s) additional dried fruit
  • 1 cup(s) raisins golden
  • 2 unit(s) lemon(s) cut into quarters, seeds removed
  • 1 quart(s) orange juice or water

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in a large saucepan. Cover and set over moderate heat until orange juice starts to simmer. Lower heat so liquid barely simmers and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the fruit is soft and most of the liquid is absorbed. Add a little more juice or water to keep the fruit moist.
  2. Store in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to 2 months.
