Chicken Veloute

Culinary Institute of America

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 3 cup(s) chicken broth heated (or veggie broth)

Directions

  1. Put flour in a small dry saute pan. Toast, stirring over low heat, until it is light brown. Transfer flour to a food processor/blender. With the machine running, pour in the broth. Pour the broth mixture into a small saucepan. Bring to a simmer over low heat, stirring frequently. Simmer the velour for 10 minutes or until thickened and smooth. Season with salt. To store, let cool, cover and refrigerate for up to 4 days.
