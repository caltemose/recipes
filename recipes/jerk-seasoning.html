Jerk Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 1 unit(s) scotch bonnet pepper(s) dried and crushed
  • 1 unit(s) allspice AKA jamaican pimento
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) crushed
  • 1 unit(s) garlic crushed
  • 1 unit(s) chili pepper dried/crushed
  • 1 unit(s) black pepper
  • 1 unit(s) celery or seeds
  • 1 unit(s) salt
  • 1 unit(s) sugar
  • 1 unit(s) paprika
  • 1 unit(s) ginger

Directions

    Notes

    In Diner's Choice version all ingredients are dried and crushed or pulverized to various levels and mixed. You could make a wetter version of this by substituting dry ingredients for fresh ones.
