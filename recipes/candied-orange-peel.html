Candied Orange Peel

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 2 unit(s) orange(s) large
  • 2 cup(s) sugar

Directions

  1. Quarter the oranges. Use a think knife to remove the pulp and save for another use. Remove white pith from peel. Slice peel into 1/4-inch strips.
  2. Move strips to a medium pan, cover with water, and boil over medium-high heat fo 15 minutes. Drain and rinse.
  3. Add strips back into pan with sugar and 2 C water and cook for 45 minutes over medium-high heat. Lower heat and cook for another 10-15 minutes until syrup reduces and dry sugar crystals begin to form. Immediately pour out the peels onto a cooling rack set over a piece of parchment. Spread the orange peel pieces into an even layer, allowing any excess sugar to fall away. Cool for 1 hour before storing. Keep any stray sugar for sweetening tea.
