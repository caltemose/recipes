Date Bars

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce(s) medjool dates pitted
  • 4 ounce(s) apricots dried
  • 1 ounce(s) ginger crystallized
  • 1 tablespoon(s) orange zest from 1/2 orange
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 2 ounce(s) pumpkin seeds green (pepitas)
  • 2.5 ounce(s) sunflower seeds raw
  • 0.75 ounce(s) coconut flakes unsweetened
  • 2 tablespoon(s) chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon(s) flaxseeds whole
  • 1 tablespoon(s) sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Line an 8x8 pan with parchment paper.
  2. Spray a food processor bowl and bald with nonstick cooking spray. Combine dates, apricots, ginger, orange zest and salt and process for 1 minute or until mixtures forms a thick paste.
  3. Heat a 12-inch skillet and cook the pipits over medium-low heat, tossing occasionally, for 1-1/2 minutes, then add sunflower seeds and continue toasting for another 5 minutes. Finally, add coconut, chia seeds, flaxseeds and sesame seeds and toast for 3 minutes or until mixture is fragrant and sunflower seeds and coconut are lightly browned.
  4. Add toasted seed mixture to date mixture in food processor and pulse 8 times, 3 seconds a pulse.
  5. Scrape the mixture into the prepared pan and press into an even layer with a rubber spatula. Refrigerate for 2 hours then cut into 12 bars with a pizza cutter. Store, separated with parchment, in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
