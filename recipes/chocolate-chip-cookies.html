Chocolate Chip Cookies

family reciple

Ingredients

  • 0.6 cup(s) vegetable shortening half butter
  • 0.25 cup(s) sugar
  • 0.25 cup(s) brown sugar
  • 1 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 1 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cup(s) all-purpose flour for softer cookies, add 1/4 C more flour
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.5 cup(s) walnuts chopped
  • 3 ounce(s) chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees. Mix shortening, sugars, egg and vanilla thoroughly. Blend dry ingredients in a separate bowl; stir into shortening mixture. Mix in nuts and chocolate chips. Drop rounded teaspoonfuls 2‚Äù apart on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes.
