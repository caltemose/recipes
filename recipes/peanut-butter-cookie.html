Peanut Butter Cookie

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 1 cup(s) peanut butter
  • 0.5 cup(s) brown sugar
  • 0.5 cup(s) sugar
  • 1 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 1 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) salt

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Line two half sheet pans with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Beat together peanut butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar until well combined with a wooden spoon in a large bowl. Add egg, baking soda, vanilla and salt and beat until well combined.
  3. Roll dough into 1-oz balls, place 8 per prepared sheet plan, then flatten with tines of a fork. Bake 10 minutes or until cookies look dry and are just lightly browned. Cool for 2 minutes on sheet pans.
