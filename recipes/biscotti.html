Biscotti

Ingredients

  • 1 cup(s) sugar
  • 0.5 cup(s) butter
  • 2 teaspoon(s) almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract
  • 1 cup(s) almonds whole, roasted, coarsely chopped
  • 3 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 2.75 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) baking powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) salt

Directions

  1. Stir together sugar, butter, and extracts in a large bowl.
  2. Add almonds and eggs and stir.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir until just combined.
  4. Chill dough, covered, 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 350F with rack in middle.
  6. Using moistened hands, halve dough and form 2 (16-by 2-inch) loaves on an ungreased large baking sheet.
  7. Bake until pale golden, about 30 minutes. Carefully transfer loaves to a rack and cool 15 minutes.
  8. Cut loaves into 3/4-inch slices with a serrated knife.
  9. Arrange biscotti, with a cut side down, on a clean baking sheet and bake until golden, 20 to 25 minutes. Transfer to rack to cool completely.

Notes

Original recipes adds 3 tablespoons of brandy. I usually don't have that and substitute some other liquor or orange extract or something similar.
