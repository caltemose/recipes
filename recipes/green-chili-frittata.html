Green Chili Frittata

Cael's mom

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon(s) baking powder
  • 10 unit(s) egg(s) lightly beaten
  • 0.25 cup(s) butter melted
  • 2 cup(s) cottage cheese small curd
  • 1 pound(s) monterey jack cheese grated
  • 1 can(s) green chilies diced, 12-ounce can
  • 1 dash salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter a 13-x baking dish. Mix flour and baking powder. Add eggs and butter; blend well. Mix in remaining ingredients and add salt to taste. Put mixture in dish and bake 35-45 minutes or until set (requires 1 hour plus to firm up). Serve hot or cold.
  2. The green chilies are really good but you can put any mixture of goodies in the frittata.
