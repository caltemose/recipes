Ricotta Pie

Italia in Cucina

Ingredients

  • 1 unit(s) Plain Pastry
  • 1.25 cup(s) ricotta
  • 2 unit(s) egg(s) beaten
  • 0.25 cup(s) ham chopped
  • 0.33 cup(s) mortadella chopped
  • 0.5 cup(s) provolone
  • 2 tablespoon(s) heavy cream or light, feel free to omit this

Directions

  1. Prepare the pastry base, roll it into a pie pan, cover it in plastic and chill it.
  2. Combine the remaining ingredients in a bowl and add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Remove the plastic from the pastry and spread the filling into the crust.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 375F for about 40 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  5. Leave to cool and serve at room temperature.

Notes

I frequently change up the meat and cheese types and usually add greens of some sort (whatever is in season). You can also make this vegetarian by substituting the meat for greens and/or other light vegetables.
