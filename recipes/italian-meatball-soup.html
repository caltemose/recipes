Italian Meatball Soup

Ingredients

  • 0.25 cup(s) olive oil
  • 1 cup(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 4 clove(s) garlic chopped
  • 1 unit(s) celery sliced thinly
  • 2 unit(s) carrot(s) sliced thinly
  • 5.5 cup(s) Chicken Broth warm
  • 2.5 cup(s) water
  • 20 unit(s) Meatballs
  • 28 ounce(s) cannellini beans or other small white beans
  • 6 ounce(s) spinach or other greens
  • 0.5 cup(s) parmeggiano grated
  • 0.75 teaspoon(s) salt (or to taste)
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) black pepper (or to taste)

Directions

    Notes

    Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a 5- to 6-quart pot over high heat until hot but not smoking and cook onions, garlic, celery, and carrots, stirring occasionally, until onions are pale golden, about 4 minutes. Stir in broth and water and bring to a boil, covered.

    Meanwhile, heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over high heat until hot but not smoking, then saute meatballs (do not thaw if frozen), turning occasionally, until browned all over, about 3 minutes. Add meatballs to soup along with beans and briskly simmer, covered, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender and meatballs are heated through, about 15 minutes.

    Stir in spinach, cheese, salt, and pepper and simmer, uncovered, until spinach is wilted, about 1 minute.
