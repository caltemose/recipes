Not Just Another Kale Salad

Everyday Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch kale lacinato/dinosaur, cut into ribbons
  • 1 bunch parsley fresh, roughly chopped
  • 1 unit(s) shallot(s) frenched
  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon(s) lemon juice fresh
  • 2 clove(s) garlic minced
  • 2 ounce(s) anchovies finely chopped
  • 3 ounce(s) feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoon(s) lemon(s) quick preserved
  • 1 cup(s) Crispy Chickpeas

Directions

  1. Toss the kale, parsley and shallot with 2 tablespoons of the olive oil and let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Puree the remaining 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the lemon juice, garlic, anchovies and 1 ounce of the feta in a food processor.
  3. Pour the dressing over the kale, toss in the preserved lemon, chickpeas and remaining feta.

Notes

The ingredient listed as 2 tablespoons of lemon should follow the Quick Preserved Lemons recipe from the same book which I have been too lazy to reproduce here.
