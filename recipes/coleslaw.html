Coleslaw

The Kitchen Survival Guide

Ingredients

  • 1 unit(s) cabbage medium head, shredded
  • 1 unit(s) red bell pepper(s) chopped
  • 1 unit(s) orange bell pepper(s) chopped
  • 2 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon(s) caraway seeds
  • 0.3 cup(s) soy sauce
  • 1.5 cup(s) mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in a large salad bowl and toss well. Serve chilled. (Suggest to make 1/2 recipe of dressing.)
