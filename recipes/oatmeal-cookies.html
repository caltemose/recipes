Oatmeal Cookies

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground cinnamon
  • 3 cup(s) rolled oats
  • 1 cup(s) sugar
  • 1 cup(s) brown sugar
  • 1 cup(s) raisins
  • 1 cup(s) vegetable oil
  • 2 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 0.3 cup(s) milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F with the rack in the center position.
  2. Sift flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon into a very large mixing bowl. Mix in the oatmeal, sugars, raisins, oil, eggs and milk. Mix very well until the ingredients are combined.
  3. Drop the batter in rounded tablespoons onto the cooking sheets. Bake for 15-20 minutes, reversing the cookies sheets once halfway through the cooking time. Remove cookies to a rack to cool.
