Stuffed Cherry Tomatoes

Williams-Sonoma Tapas cookbook

Ingredients

  • 24 unit(s) cherry tomatoes
  • 1 unit(s) anchovy and olive filling
  • 1 unit(s) black olive and caper filling

Directions

  1. If necessary, cut and discard a very thin slice from the stalk end of each tomato to make the base flat and stable. Cut a thin slice from the smooth end of each tomato and discard. Using a serrated knife or teaspoon, loosen the pulp and seeds of each and scoop out, discarding the flesh. Turn the scooped-out tomatoes upside down on paper towels and let drain for 5 minutes.
  2. Prepare the preferred filling.
  3. Fill a pastry bag with a 3/4-inch plain tip with the filling and use to pack the filling into hollow tomato shells. Store the tomatoes in the fridge until ready to serve.
