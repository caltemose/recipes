Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup(s) butter unsalted
  • 1 cup(s) peanut butter
  • 1 cup(s) sugar
  • 1 cup(s) brown sugar
  • 2 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 2.5 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon(s) baking powder
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt

Directions

    Notes

    Cream together butter, peanut butter and sugars. Beat in eggs. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir into batter. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour. Roll into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a criss-cross pattern. Bake in a preheated 375F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown. Do not over-bake.
