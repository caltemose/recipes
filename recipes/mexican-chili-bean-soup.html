Mexican Chili Bean Soup

Soup cookbook

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon(s) sunflower oil
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 1 unit(s) red bell pepper(s) seeded, finely chopped
  • 2 clove(s) garlic finely chopped
  • 14 ounce(s) ground turkey
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoon(s) chili powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) oregano dried
  • 1 can(s) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoon(s) tomato puree
  • 3.25 cup(s) vegetable stock hot
  • 1 can(s) kidney beans drained and rinsed
  • 1 unit(s) parsley chopped, to serve
  • 1 unit(s) sour cream to serve
  • 1 unit(s) cheddar grated, to serve

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan, add the onion, and cook for 5 minutes. Add the pepper and garlic and stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add the turkey and stir-fry for 5 minutes or until it has broken up and browned. Stir in cumin, chili powder and oregano and cook for another minute or so.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, tomato puree and stock, then simmer for 20-25 minutes until reduced slightly and a good soupy consistency. Add beans and simmer for another 5 minutes. Stir in half the parsley and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Ladle into bowl and serve topped with sour cream, cheddar, and parsley.
