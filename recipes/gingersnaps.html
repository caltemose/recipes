Gingersnaps

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 0.75 cup(s) vegetable shortening
  • 1 cup(s) brown sugar
  • 0.25 cup(s) molasses
  • 1 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 2.25 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground cinnamon
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) ground cloves

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cream together first 4 ingredients until fluffy. Sift together dry ingredients in a separate bowl; stir into molasses mixture. Form into small balls. Roll in granulated sugar; place 2 inches apart on greased cooking sheet. Bake about 10 minutes.
