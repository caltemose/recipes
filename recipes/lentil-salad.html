Lentil Salad

Williams-Sonoma Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 cup(s) lentil(s)
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon(s) red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove(s) garlic
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) ground cumin
  • 1 unit(s) red bell pepper(s)
  • 10 ounce(s) feta cheese 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 unit(s) cucumber(s) peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 10 unit(s) kalamata olive(s) quartered

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, cover lentils with 2 inches water. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce head to medium-low and simmer, uncovered, until lentils are tender, 15-20 minutes. Remove from heat and drain.
  2. Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, cumin, and garlic.
  3. In a bowl, mix lentils and veggies. Spoon on dressing and mix. Add olives and feta.
