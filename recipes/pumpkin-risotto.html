Pumpkin Risotto

Ingredients

  • 10 ounce(s) pumpkin cleaned and diced
  • 1 unit(s) garlic finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 3 tablespoon(s) butter
  • 2 cup(s) arborio rice
  • 1 tablespoon(s) parsley
  • 4 unit(s) Chicken Broth or beef or veg stock
  • 4 tablespoon(s) parmeggiano

Directions

    Notes

    Put the pumpkin in a large, heavy-bottomed saucepan with the garlic, oil and half the butter. Saut√©, stirring, over medium heat for 8-10 minutes. Add the rice and cook for 2 minutes, stirring continuously. Add the boiling stock gradually, stirring frequently. The pumpkin will start to disintegrate. The rice will cook in about 15-18 minutes. It should be quite creamy when ready. Sir in the remaining butter, the parsley, parmeggiano, salt and pepper and serve immediately.
