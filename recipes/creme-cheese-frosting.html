Creme Cheese Frosting

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 ounce(s) cream cheese room temperature
  • 0.25 cup(s) butter room temperature
  • 2 cup(s) powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Place cream cheese and butter in a mixing bowl and beat until very smooth. Place sugar in a fine-mesh strainer and sift over the cream cheese mixture.&nbsp; Beat by hand until light and fluffy. Mix in the vanilla.
