Thai Ginger Chicken Soup

Keo's Thai Cusine

Ingredients

  • 0.5 pound(s) chicken whatever boneless, skinless, cut into strips
  • 3 cup(s) coconut milk
  • 2 cup(s) water
  • 1 piece(s) ginger about an inch, sliced thinly
  • 1 tablespoon(s) fish sauce to taste
  • 3 unit(s) lime(s) juiced
  • 2 tablespoon(s) scallions sliced
  • 1 tablespoon(s) parsley Chinese parsley or similar

Directions

  1. Bring coconut milk and water to a light boil and reduce to medium low.
  2. Add chicken and cook 3 minutes.
  3. Stir in ginger, fish sauce and lime juice. Sprinkle with scallion and parsley and serve hot.

Notes

Add hot stuff to taste if desired. I'll add some slices of thai chili if I have them.

The amount of fish sauce you add is up to your taste. If you haven't used it before, go light, it's potent. Or just omit - whatever.

I don't follow this recipe exactly - I adjust it for 1 can of coconut milk which is almost 2 cups. I may also substitute shrimp for chicken and add some thin slices of carrot or other vegetables depending on what I have. I might even add noodles. This soup with just chicken is not super exciting but with modifications is pretty nice.
