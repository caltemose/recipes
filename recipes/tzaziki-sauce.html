Tzaziki Sauce

Culinary Institute of America

Ingredients

  • 1 unit(s) cucumber(s)
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 cup(s) yogurt plain
  • 1 cup(s) yogurt yogurt cheese
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) garlic minced, then pureed in a mortar
  • 2 tablespoon(s) mint fresh, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon(s) lemon juice to taste

Directions

  1. Combine cucumber and salt in a colander and let it drain for 15 minutes. Squeeze out the excess moisture. Put cucumber in a medium bowl and combine with yogurt, yogurt cheese, garlic, mint and lemon juice. Adjust the seasoning with salt if necessary.
