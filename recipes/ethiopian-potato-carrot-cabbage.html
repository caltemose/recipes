Ethiopian Potato Carrot Cabbage

Nom Nom Paleo

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon(s) ghee
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) diced
  • 1 unit(s) cabbage green, cored and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 4 unit(s) carrot(s) cut into 1-inch rounds
  • 3 teaspoon(s) salt divided
  • 1.75 pound(s) potato cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 teaspoon(s) turmeric ground
  • 1 teaspoon(s) cumin seeds ground
  • 1 teaspoon(s) black pepper ground

Directions

  1. Heat the ghee and olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onions, cabbage and carrots and 2 teaspoons salt.
  3. Stir and cover the pot. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the potatoes, remaining salt, turmeric, cumin and pepper and stir well.
  5. Cover and reduce heat to medium low. Cook for 30 minutes or until all vegetables are tender.
