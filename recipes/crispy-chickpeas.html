Crispy Chickpeas

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 2 can(s) chickpeas 30 oz total
  • 1 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) sumac
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Rinse the chickpeas under cold water in a salad spinner. Drain, then spin to dry even more. Move to a paper-towel-lined half sheet pan. Top with another layer of paper towels, roll up and pat to dry even more.
  2. Remove paper towels from sheet pan and toss the chickpeas with olive oil and salt. Put the pan with chickpeas in a cold over, set a timer for 30 minutes, and crank the oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Turn the oven off and leave the chickpeas inside to dry and become crispy, about 1 hour.
  4. Toss the still-warm chickpeas with the sumac and cayenne pepper. Cool completely before storing.
