Crispy Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 30 ounce(s) chickpeas two 15-ounce cans (cooked)
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) sumac
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Rinse the chickpeas under cold water. Dry well (salad spinner + paper towels, etc).
  2. Toss the chickpeas with oil and salt. Place on a sheet pan.
  3. Put the pan in a cold oven and turn the oven to 350F.
  4. Turn the oven off after 30 minutes and leave the chickpeas in the oven for another hour.
  5. Toss the chickpeas with sumac and cayenne. Cool completely before storing.
