Pumpkin Polenta

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 4 leave(s) sage fresh, chopped
  • 2 cup(s) Chicken Broth
  • 2 cup(s) milk
  • 0.666 cup(s) polenta
  • 1.5 cup(s) pumpkin pulp, fresh or canned
  • 0.25 cup(s) parmeggiano grated

Directions

    Notes

    Heat the olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add the sage to the oil and cook until the sage is slightly crisped, about 1 minute. Remove from the heat and set aside. Meanwhile, put the broth and milk in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Slowly whisk in the polenta, reduce the heat to low, and cook, whisking occasionally, until the polenta is thick and creamy, about 25 minutes. Pull the saucepan from the heat and whisk in the pumpkin, sage with the oil, cheese, salt, and pepper. Hold in a warm place.
