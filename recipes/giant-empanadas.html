Giant Empanadas

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can(s) kidney beans
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 1 unit(s) red bell pepper(s) chopped
  • 1 can(s) canned tomatoes chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground cumin
  • 2 unit(s) pie crust
  • 1 unit(s) egg yolks mixed w/ 1 T water
  • 1 unit(s) sour cream
  • 1 unit(s) lettuce shredded

Directions

  1. Cook onion and bell pepper in skillet, 5 minutes.&nbsp; Stir in beans, tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper. Simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes.
  2. Place 1 unfolded pie crust at one end of greased cookie sheet. Place half of filling on half of pastry round, leaving 1-inch border. Fold other half of pastry over filling. Press edges to seal. Brush pastry with yolk mixture. Repeat with other pie crust.
  3. Bake in pre-heated 400 degree F oven for 20 minutes until pastry is golden and filling hot. Cut into halves and garnish with sour cream and shredded lettuce.
