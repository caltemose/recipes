Cucumber and Onion Salad

Italia in Cucina

Ingredients

  • 5 unit(s) red onion(s)
  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon(s) white wine vinegar
  • 2 unit(s) cucumber(s)
  • 1 tablespoon(s) capers
  • 6 leave(s) basil torn

Directions

  1. Peel the onions and slice in thin wheels.
  2. Put the onions in a salad bowl, sprinkle with salt, pepper, the olive oil and vinegar. Toss well and set aside for 30 minutes.
  3. Peel the cucumbers and slice thinly.
  4. &nbsp;Add the cucumbers and capers to the onions and toss well.
  5. Garnish with basil and serve.
