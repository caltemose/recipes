Overnight Oats

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup(s) coconut milk
  • 0.3 cup(s) almond milk
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) vanilla extract
  • 0.5 cup(s) rolled oats
  • 0.3 cup(s) dried fruit
  • 1 tablespoon(s) chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon(s) flaxseed meal
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 unit(s) coconut flakes toasted
  • 1 unit(s) almonds slivered and toasted

Directions

  1. In a 16-oz jar, add oats, fruit, chia seeds, flax meal, salt and cinnamon.
  2. Add coconut milk, almond milk and vanilla. Shake jar to combine. Refrigerate overnight.
  3. Serve in the jar, topped with coconut and/or almonds.
