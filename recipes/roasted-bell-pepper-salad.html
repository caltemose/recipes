Roasted Bell Pepper Salad

Williams-Sonoma Tapas cookbook

Ingredients

  • 3 unit(s) red bell pepper(s)
  • 3 unit(s) yellow bell peppers
  • 5 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon(s) dry sherry vinegar or lemon juice
  • 2 clove(s) garlic crushed
  • 1 tablespoon(s) capers
  • 8 unit(s) black olives
  • 2 tablespoon(s) marjoram chopped, plus extra sprigs to garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat the broiler. Cut the bell peppers in half, place skin-side up on a cookie sheet, and cook under a hot broiler for 10 minutes, until their skins have blackened and blistered.
  2. Remove the roasted peppers, put them in a bowl, and immediately cover with a damp kitchen towel. Let the peppers stand for about 15 minutes.
  3. Peel the skin off the peppers and cut them into neat thin strips. Arrange the strips attractively on a serving dish. Save any juices that come off the peppers.
  4. To the pepper juices, add the olive oil, sherry vinegar, garlic, and salt and pepper.&nbsp; Whisk together until combined. Drizzle the dressing over the salad.
  5. Sprinkle the capers, olives and chopped marjoram over the salad, garnish with marjoram sprigs, and serve at room temperature.
