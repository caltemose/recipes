Plain Pastry

Italia in Cucina

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 2 salt
  • 1 unit(s) egg(s) just the yolk
  • 0.5 cup(s) butter sliced thinly
  • 2 tablespoon(s) water

Directions

  1. Combine the flour and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Make a hollow in the center and fill with egg, butter and water.
  3. Mix ingredients, mashing with a fork as you work.
  4. Transfer crumbly dough to a floured work surface and shape into a compact ball, kneading as little as possible.
  5. Chill or use as needed.

Notes

I find it easier to mix the wet ingredients and add flour as you go to keep from over-flouring the dough. You can chill this dough in plastic before using or, as the original recipe suggests, form it in a pie pan (or whatever you're using) and then chill it.
