Potato Salad

The Kitchen Survival Guide

Ingredients

  • 3 pound(s) potato cut into pieces
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) finely chopped
  • 2 stalk(s) celery
  • 2 tablespoon(s) parsley chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) dill chopped
  • 2 tablespoon(s) red wine vinegar or white wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) pepper
  • 1 tablespoon(s) Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup(s) mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Boil potatoes in enough salted water to cover for about 20 minutes, or until they are tender. Drain and let cool.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Toss to mix well and chill before serving.
