Sizzling Chili Shrimp

Williams-Sonoma Tapas cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1 pound(s) jumbo shrimp raw, in shells
  • 1 unit(s) red chiles small, fresh
  • 6 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 clove(s) garlic finely chopped
  • 1 pinch paprika
  • 1

Directions

  1. To prepare the shrimp, remove the head and legs, leaving the shells and tails intact. Using a short knife, make a shallow slit along the underside of each shrimp, then pull out the dark being and discard. Rinse the shrimp under cold water and dry well on paper towels.
  2. Cut the chili in half lengthwise, remove the seed, and finely chop the flesh.&nbsp;&nbsp;
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large, heavy-bottom skillet until quite hot, then add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the shrimp, chili, paprika and a pinch of salt and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring all the time, until the shrimp turn pink and start to color.
  4. Serve in the cooking dish, still sizzling. Accompany with crusty bread to mop up the cooking oil.
