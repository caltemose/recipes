Gazpacho - Juli

personal recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 unit(s) tomato(es) medium size, blanched and peeled, seeds removed, coarsely chopped
  • 1 unit(s) cucumber(s) peeled, seeds removed, coarsely chopped
  • 1 unit(s) red bell pepper(s) roasted, peeled, ribs and seeds removed, coarsely chopped
  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil plus more to taste
  • 2 tablespoon(s) red wine vinegar plus more to taste
  • 1 unit(s) salt to taste

Directions

  1. Add veggies to a blender and process until smooth. Add 4 T of olive oil and blend. Taste to see if you like the flavor or if you want to add more olive oil. Add 2 T of vinegar. Taste to see if you want to add more. (Remember that tomatoes and vinegar are both acidic, so too much vinegar can make the soup too acidic. This is why I taste as I go and donâ€™t just dump all of the vinegar in at once.) Add salt which will really open up the flavors.
  2. Let the soup sit in the fridge for a couple of days so the flavors can blend. Serve chilled or at room temperature. This can be a light main course served with some crusty bread on the side or it can be a cup of soup to accompany other dishes.
