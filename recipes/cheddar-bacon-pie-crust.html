Cheddar Bacon Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 1.25 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 2 tablespoon(s) butter cold, cut into bits
  • 1.5 cup(s) cheddar grated
  • 4 slice(s) bacon cooked crisp and crumbled
  • 1.5 cup(s) milk

Directions

  1. In a bowl sift together the flour, the baking powder, and the salt, add the butter, and blend the mixture until it resembles meal.
  2. Stir in the Cheddar and the bacon and stir in enough of the milk to form a soft, sticky dough.
