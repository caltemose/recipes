Egg Salad

The Kitchen Survival Guide

Ingredients

  • 4 unit(s) egg(s) hard-boiled
  • 3 tablespoon(s) mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon(s) Dijon mustard
  • 1 stalk(s) celery finely chopped
  • 6 unit(s) green olive(s) chopped
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) curry powder optional

Directions

  1. Place eggs in medium-sized bowl and use two knives in a crisscross motion to break them into small pieces. Stir in the other ingredients and mix to blend.
