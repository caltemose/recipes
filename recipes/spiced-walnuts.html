Spiced Walnuts

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon(s) water
  • 1 tablespoon(s) honey
  • 2 teaspoon(s) olive oil
  • 2 cup(s) walnuts halves
  • 1 tablespoon(s) sugar
  • 0.15 teaspoon(s) cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground cinnamon
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) ground ginger

Directions

  1. Warm liquids over medium heat.
  2. Add nuts and toss well to coat.
  3. Add spices and toss to coat.
  4. Mix until nuts are toasted, 3-5 minutes.
  5. Spread in one layer on a baking sheet and let cool.&nbsp;&nbsp;
