Cream of Mushroom Soup

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 0.25 pound(s) mushrooms
  • 0.5 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 2 tablespoon(s) butter
  • 2 tablespoon(s) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cup(s) broth
  • 0.5 cup(s) half and half
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) pepper
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) nutmeg

Directions

  1. Slice mushrooms through cap and stem; cook with onion in butter 5 minutes. Add flour and blend; add stock. Cook and stir till slightly thickened. Cool slightly; add cream and seasonings. Heat through; serve at once.
