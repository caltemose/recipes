Black Beans

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 4 clove(s) garlic smashed
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) red pepper flakes
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 can(s) black beans drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon(s) oregano dried
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) black pepper

Directions

  1. Place 12-inch saute pan over medium heat. Add olive oil, garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring frequently, until garlic is golden brown, 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add onion and salt and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring often, until onion starts to color. Add black beans, oregano and black pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, for 6-8 minutes.
