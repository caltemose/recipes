Couscous - Stovetop

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon(s) butter
  • 1 cup(s) broth
  • 1 cup(s) couscous

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan. Stir in couscous, cover pan and remove from heat. Let stand for 5 minutes and check to see if all liquid has been absorbed. If not, cover and let stand a few more minutes.
