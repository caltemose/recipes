Butternut Squash-Carrot Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 1 unit(s) yellow onion(s) chopped
  • 1 unit(s) butternut squash about 1.25 lb, coarsely chopped
  • 3 unit(s) carrot(s) coarsely chopped
  • 1.25 teaspoon(s) cumin seeds ground
  • 0.75 teaspoon(s) turmeric
  • 0.75 teaspoon(s) coriander seeds ground
  • 4 cup(s) vegetable stock

Directions

  1. In a 4-qt pot over medium-low head, warm olive oil.&nbsp; Add the onion and saut√©, stirring occasionally, until soft, about 10 minutes.&nbsp; Add squash, carrots and sauce, stirring, for 10 minutes.&nbsp; Add the spices and continue to sauce, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes longer.&nbsp; Add stock and bring to a boil over high heat.&nbsp; Reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, until squash and carrots are soft, 30-40 minutes.&nbsp; Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  2. Working with 2 C of soup at a time, blend on high speed until very smooth and light.&nbsp; If soup is too thick, thin with a little water.
  3. Place soup over medium-high heat and warm to serving temperature.&nbsp; Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Ladle into soup bowls and garnish.
