Perfect French Omelette

Great Courses

Ingredients

  • 2 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 1 tablespoon(s) butter
  • 1 teaspoon(s) chive(s) fresh, chopped

Directions

  1. Beat eggs in a small cup/bowl.
  2. Melt butter in a small saucepan.
  3. Add egg mixture to saucepan. Let sit for 1-2 minutes, then use a heat-proof spatula to pull the edges of the eggs away from the sides of the pan to let the runny part of the eggs run under the cooked edges.&nbsp;
  4. Let cook for a few minutes. You want the egg mixture on top to firm up (not be liquid) but you donâ€™t want the bottom to become too brown.&nbsp;
  5. When the top is almost firmed up, season with salt and pepper. If you want to add ham, cheese, etc, do it now!&nbsp; (Although a traditional French omelette does not have these extra ingredients.)
  6. Knock the side of the pan a couple of times with the palm of your hand to loosen the omelette from the bottom of the pan and keep it from sticking.
  7. When the omelette is fully cooked, fold 1/3 over the middle. Then roll the omelette onto the plate by folding the middle over the remaining third and onto the plate. Garnish with chopped chives. Serve with a green salad and some crusty bread.
