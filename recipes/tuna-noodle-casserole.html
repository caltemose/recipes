Tuna Noodle Casserole

family recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 ounce(s) egg noodles
  • 6 ounce(s) tuna canned
  • 0.5 cup(s) mayonnaise
  • 1 cup(s) celery sliced
  • 0.3 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 can(s) cream of celery soup
  • 0.5 cup(s) milk
  • 1 cup(s) cheddar
  • 0.5 cup(s) almonds slivered and toasted

Directions

  1. Cook noodles in boiling salted water until tender.&nbsp; Drain. Combine noodles, drained tuna, mayonnaise, vegetables and salt.
  2. <div>Blend soup and milk; heat through. Add cheese; heat and stir till cheese melts. Add to noodle mixture. Turn into 1-1/2-quart casserole. Top with almonds. Bake at 425 degrees F for 20 minutes.</div><div><br></div>
