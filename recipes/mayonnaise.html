Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon(s) lemon juice
  • 1 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon(s) white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon(s) Dijon mustard
  • 1.75 cup(s) avocado oil roughly

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a pint mason jar and mix with an immersion blender until proper consistency - 30 seconds or so.

Notes

The measurement of oil is a guess. After adding everything else, fill the mason jar to about 95% and then mix.

You can use any mustard or mustard powder. You can also omit it and substitute many other things to taste.
