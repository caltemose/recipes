Curd Cheese and Herb Filling

Williams-Sonoma Tapas cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1 cup(s) curd cheese
  • 1 teaspoon(s) lemon juice
  • 1 clove(s) garlic crushed
  • 4 tablespoon(s) parsley chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) mint chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) oregano fresh, chopped

Directions

  1. Mix and use as a filling for stuffed pimientos.
