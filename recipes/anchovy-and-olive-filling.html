Anchovy and Olive Filling

Williams-Sonoma Tapas cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1.75 ounce(s) anchovies in olive oil, drained and finely chopped
  • 1 splash(es) anchovy oil from the can, to moisten the filling
  • 8 unit(s) green olive(s) pimiento stuffed, finely chopped
  • 2 unit(s) egg(s) large, hardboiled, finely chopped

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together as a filling for stuffed cherry tomatoes.
