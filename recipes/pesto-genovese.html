Pesto Genovese

Ingredients

  • 2 cup(s) basil genovese small basil is best
  • 2 tablespoon(s) pine nuts
  • 1 clove(s) garlic
  • 0.5 cup(s) olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon(s) parmeggiano grated
  • 2 tablespoon(s) pecorino grated
  • 2 tablespoon(s) water from the pasta pot
  • 1 tablespoon(s) butter

Directions

    Notes

    Combine the basil, pine nuts, garlic, oil and salt in a food processor and chop until smooth. Place the mixture in a large bowl and stir in the cheeses. Add the water and butter and stir well.
