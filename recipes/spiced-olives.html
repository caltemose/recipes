Spiced Olives

Ingredients

  • 10 ounce(s) olive(s) pitted
  • 4 clove(s) garlic peeled
  • 1 sprig(s) rosemary fresh
  • 1 tablespoon(s) mint finely chopped
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) oregano
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) chili pepper crushed
  • 2 tablespoon(s) olive oil

Directions

    Notes

    Rinse the olives in cold water and pat dry. Lightly crush the olives with a mallet. Do the same with the garlic. Place the olives and garlic in a serving dish. Remove the rosemary leaves from the sprig and add to the olives, together with the mint, oregano, chillies and oil. Mix well and cover. Set aside in a cool place (not the refrigerator) for at least 2 hours before serving. Serve with crusty bread.
