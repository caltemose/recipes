Five-Spice Powder

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon(s) cinnamon
  • 6 unit(s) star anise
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) fennel seeds
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) Szechuan peppercorns
  • 0.75 teaspoon(s) cloves ground

Directions

    Notes

    Blend ingredients in grinder until finely ground. Store in airtight container - keeps up to 2 months.
