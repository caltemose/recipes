Chilled Curried Potato-Leek Soup

Williams-Sonoma Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon(s) butter unsalted
  • 2 unit(s) yellow onion(s) chopped
  • 3 unit(s) leek(s) white part and 1 inch of green, halved lengthwise, carefully washed and sliced crosswise
  • 1 clove(s) garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon(s) curry powder
  • 3 cup(s) vegetable stock
  • 1 pound(s) red potato(es) peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 3 cup(s) milk
  • 2 tablespoon(s) chive(s) fresh, chopped for garnish

Directions

  1. In a 4-qt pot over medium-low heat, melt butter. Add onions and leeks and saute, stirring, until vegetables begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and curry powder and sauce, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are soft, about 10 minutes. Add stock, potatoes and 1 t salt. Cover and cook until potatoes are soft when pierced with a fork, about 20 minutes.
  2. Pour in milk and stir. Working with 2 C soup at a time, blend until very smooth. If soup is too thick, add more milk. Cover and chill for 2 hours.
  3. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls and garnish.
