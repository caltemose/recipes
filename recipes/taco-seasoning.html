Taco Seasoning

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoon(s) chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) ground cumin
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) red pepper flakes crushed
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) garlic powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) onion powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) oregano dried
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) black pepper

Directions

  1. Mix ingredients; Makes enough for 1 pound ground meat; 5 teaspoons, 2 tablespoons.
