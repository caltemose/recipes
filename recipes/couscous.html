Couscous

Chad's Italian cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup(s) couscous
  • 2.25 cup(s) water boiling
  • 2 tablespoon(s) butter unsalted
  • 0.75 teaspoon(s)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees.
  2. In a 9x12 baking dish (pyrex), pour in the couscous in an even layer.
