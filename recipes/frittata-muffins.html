Frittata Muffins

Nom Nom Paleo

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoon(s) ghee
  • 0.5 unit(s) onion(s) finely diced
  • 0.5 pound(s) mushrooms thinly sliced
  • 0.5 pound(s) spinach frozen (or other greens)
  • 8 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 0.25 cup(s) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoon(s) coconut flour
  • 1 cup(s) cherry tomato(es) halved
  • 5 ounce(s) ham or prosciutto, etc

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Heat the ghee and saut√© onions until translucent.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until moisture evaporates.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and cool to room temperature.
  5. Beat the eggs with the coconut milk, coconut flour and salt and pepper to taste until mixed.
  6. Grease two muffin tins and line each cup with ham, taking care to cover the bottom and sides completely.
  7. Fill each cup about 3/4 full and top each muffin with the halved cherry tomatoes.
  8. Bake for about 20 minutes, flipping the tray after 10.
  9. Cool the muffins in the pan for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

Notes

The original recipe adds 3 minced garlic cloves and suggests prosciutto over ham. I use ham as it's usually easier to cover my muffin tins.
