Tuna Salad

The Kitchen Survival Guide

Ingredients

  • 1 can(s) tuna
  • 3 tablespoon(s) mayonnaise
  • 2 stalk(s) celery finely minced
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) sweet, small, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) capers or more to taste

Directions

  1. Drain tuna. Place tuna in a medium-size mixing bowl and mash it with a fork. Stir in mayonnaise and then the celery, onion, and capers.
