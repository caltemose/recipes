Coucous Tomato Basil Salad

Epicurious

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cup(s) Chicken Broth
  • 10 ounce(s) couscous
  • 1 cup(s) scallions chopped
  • 1 cup(s) tomato(es) seeded and diced
  • 0.333 cup(s) basil sliced thinly
  • 0.5 cup(s) olive oil
  • 0.25 cup(s) balsamic vinegar
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) chili pepper crushed
  • 1 pint(s) cherry tomato(es) halved

Directions

  1. Bring broth to boil in medium saucepan.
  2. Add couscous. Remove from heat. Cover and let rest 5 minutes.
  3. Transfer to large bowl. Fluff with fork. Cool.
  4. Mix all ingredients except cherry tomatoes into couscous. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Garnish with cherry tomatoes.

Notes

If you have a preferred method for making couscous that differs from the instructions here, use it. No worries.
