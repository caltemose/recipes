Coucous Tomato Basil Salad

Ingredients

  • 2.25 cup(s) Chicken Broth
  • 10 ounce(s) couscous
  • 1 cup(s) scallions chopped
  • 1 cup(s) tomato(es) seeded and diced
  • 0.333 cup(s) basil sliced thinly
  • 0.5 cup(s) olive oil
  • 0.25 cup(s) balsamic vinegar
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) chili pepper crushed
  • 1 pint(s) cherry tomato(es) halved

Directions

    Notes

    Bring broth to boil in medium saucepan. Add couscous. Remove from heat. Cover; let stand 5 minutes. Transfer to large bowl. Fluff with fork. Cool. Mix all ingredients except cherry tomatoes into couscous. Season with salt and pepper. (Can be made 1 day ahead. Chill.) Garnish with cherry tomatoes.
