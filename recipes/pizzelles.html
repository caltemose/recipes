Pizzelles

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon(s) sugar
  • 3 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 3 tablespoon(s) vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoon(s) anise seeds vary amount to taste
  • 0.5 cup(s) all-purpose flour vary amount to make runny dough

Directions

    Notes

    Beat eggs lightly with vegetable oil. Mix in sugar and anise seeds. Then mix in flour until you have a runny dough about the same consistency as pancake batter. Rub oil into a pizzelle iron, heat it up and then cook about a teaspoon of dough at a time. Cook time per pizzelle will decrease once the iron is at full temperature. Longer cooking makes crunchier pizzelles.
