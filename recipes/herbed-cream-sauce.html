Herbed Cream Sauce

Culinary Institute of America

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon(s) butter unsalted
  • 3 unit(s) shallot(s) minced
  • 1 clove(s) garlic minced
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) paprika
  • 0.25 cup(s) white wine dry
  • 1 cup(s) heavy cream
  • 0.3 cup(s) fresh herbs basil, dill, parsley, chervil, tarragon and/or chives - chopped

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and saute shallots, garlic and paprika until fragrant. Add the wine and cook until reduced by half. Gradually whisk in the velour and simmer for 5 minutes. Strain the sauce. Add the cream and herbs. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Keep warm until serving.
