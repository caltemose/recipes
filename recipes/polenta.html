Polenta

Ingredients

  • 4 cup(s) water or broth
  • 1 cup(s) corn coarse corn meal
  • 2 teaspoon(s) salt or so

Directions

  1. Boil the water/broth in a large, heavy-bottomed pot. Salt it sufficiently to flavor the polenta.
  2. Add the cornmeal gradually, whisking continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Stir almost continuously for 50-60 minutes.

Notes

This is the basic recipe. However, the best way to prepare polenta is to have about twice as much broth (broth is always better than water in this case) and start the recipe as noted. As the polenta cooks and the liquid reduces, add more broth by the 1/3-1/2 cupful. After doing this for quite some time, the polenta will eventually stop absorbing the additional broth. If you can get a good hour or more doing this, your polenta will improve greatly over the default recipe.
