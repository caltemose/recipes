Yogurt Cheese

Culinary Institute of America

Ingredients

  • 1 quart(s) yogurt plain

Directions

  1. Place 1 quart plain yogurt in a cheesecloth-lined sieve in a bowl deep enough to allow 1 inch between bottom of sieve and bottom of bowl. Fold cheesecloth over the top and place a small weight on the yogurt. Refrigerate and let drain overnight.
