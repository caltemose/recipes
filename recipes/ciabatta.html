Ciabatta

Great British Baking Show

Ingredients

  • 500 gram(s) all-purpose flour or bread flour, high gluten is best
  • 10 gram(s) yeast
  • 10 gram(s) salt
  • 440 milliliter(s) water

Directions

  1. Put flour into mixing bowl. Put yeast on one side and salt on the other.
  2. Add 70-80% of the water and mix using the dough hook. (Kitchenaid speed 1 then 2)
  3. Add remaining water as the dough forms. Knead in the mixer for about 15 minutes.
  4. Oil a 3-liter plastic tub (rectangle/square) and put the dough in it.
  5. Cover the dough and let it proof until it has doubled in size (1.5-2 hours).
  6. Add flour and corn meal to your work surface and gently pour the dough out of the container.
  7. Cover the dough with semolina and shape it into a square. Cut the dough in half and using the cutting instrument (knife or dough scraper) fold one of the loaves over and away from the other half.
  8. Add flour and semolina to a baking tray and put both loaves on the tray. Let the dough rest for 30-45 minutes. Preheat the oven to 430F convection/fan (or 460F without fan).
  9. Bake the loaves for 25 minutes - check after 20.
