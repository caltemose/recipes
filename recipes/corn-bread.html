Corn Bread

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup(s) butter
  • 0.667 cup(s) sugar
  • 2 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 1 cup(s) buttermilk
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 1 cup(s) corn meal
  • 1 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F. Grease an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Melt butter in sauce pan. Remove from heat and stir in sugar. Add eggs and beat until well blended.
  3. Combine buttermilk with baking soda and stir mixture into pan.
  4. Combine remaining dry ingredients in bowl and mix.
  5. Combine wet and dry mixtures and mix.
  6. Pour batter into the greased pan and bake for 30-40 minutes.

Notes

You can also do this the old-fashioned way in a cast iron pan by starting it on the stove and finishing it in the oven.
