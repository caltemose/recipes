Crepes

Ingredients

  • 0.75 cup(s) milk cold
  • 0.75 cup(s) water cold
  • 3 unit(s) egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon(s) sugar
  • 1 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 5 tablespoon(s) butter melted

Directions

  1. Put liquids, eggs and salt into blender jar. Add flour then butter. Cover and blend at top speed for 1 minute. If bits of flour adhere to side of jar, dislodge with a scraper and blend for 2-3 seconds more. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  2. The batter should be a very light cream, just thick enough to coat a wooden spoon. If, after making your first crepe, it seems too heavy, beat in a bit of water, a spoonful at a time. Your cooked crepe should be about 1/16 inch thick.
  3. Place cooked crepe on serving dish. Cover with wax paper and a top lid to keep crepes from drying out.
