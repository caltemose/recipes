Savory Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 cup(s) bread stale, cubed
  • 3 cup(s) ham cubed
  • 6 ounce(s) cheddar cubed
  • 8 ounce(s) mushrooms sliced and sauteed
  • 0.75 cup(s) spinach chopped
  • 1.5 unit(s) broccoli heads, roasted
  • 8 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 2 cup(s) milk
  • 1 dash hot sauce
  • 0.5 cup(s) parmeggiano grated

Directions

  1. Butter a 9x13 pan.&nbsp; Layer the bread on the bottom.&nbsp; Then layer mushrooms, spinach or broccoli, and ham.&nbsp; Top with cheddar and parmesan.&nbsp;&nbsp;
  2. Mix eggs, milk and hot sauce in a bowl and then pour over the dish.&nbsp; Cover and refrigerate overnight.
