Stuffed Pimientos

Williams-Sonoma Tapas cookbook

Ingredients

  • 6.5 ounce(s) pimientos del piquillo canned, whole, or substitute pappadew
  • 8 sprig(s) fresh herbs to garnish
  • 1 unit(s) curd cheese and herb filling
  • 1 unit(s) tuna mayonnaise filling
  • 1 unit(s) goat cheese and olive filling

Directions

  1. Prepare preferred filling by mixing all ingredients well.
  2. Using a teaspoon, heap the filling of your choice into each pimiento. Put in the refrigerator and let chill for at least 2 hours until firm.
  3. To serve, arrange peppers on a serving plate and garnish with herb sprigs.
