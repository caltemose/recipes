Corn Soup

Ingredients

  • 0.25 unit(s) butter
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 0.5 cup(s) bell pepper(s) diced
  • 0.5 cup(s) red bell pepper(s) diced
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) cumin seeds ground
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) cayenne pepper ground
  • 0.25 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup(s) Chicken Broth
  • 1 cup(s) milk
  • 1 pound(s) corn kernels

Directions

  1. Melt butter in heavy large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, red and green bell peppers, cumin and cayenne and saut√© until vegetables are tender, about 8 minutes.
  3. Add flour and stir 1 minute.
  4. Gradually mix in broth, milk and reserved corn liquid. Bring mixture to boil, whisking until smooth.
  5. Mix in corn and stir until corn is just heated through.
  6. Ladle soup into bowls. Garnish with cilantro if desired and serve.
