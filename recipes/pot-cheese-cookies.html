Pot Cheese Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 pound(s) ricotta
  • 0.75 pound(s) butter
  • 3 tablespoon(s) vanilla extract
  • 3 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 3 cup(s) sugar
  • 6 cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) baking soda

Directions

    Notes

    Beat first 5 ingredients together in mixer. Combine and mix remaining ingredients then slowly add to mixture in mixer. Drop by 1/2 teaspoon onto greased cookie sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes at 375Â°F. Optionally ice with basic milk/water/lemon juice/etc plus powdered sugar icing.
