Wholemeal Rolls

Soup cookbook

Ingredients

  • 1.6 cup(s) bread flour plus more for kneading
  • 1.6 cup(s) whole wheat flour
  • 0.25 ounce(s) yeast
  • 2 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.75 cup(s) milk plus 2 T, tepid whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon(s) honey
  • 1 tablespoon(s) vegetable oil
  • 1 unit(s) egg(s) beaten, to glaze
  • 1 unit(s) rolled oats to sprinkle

Directions

  1. Mix the flours, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.&nbsp; Stir the milk, water, oil and honey together, then pour into a well in the flours. Stir until slightly sticky. Cover with plastic wrap and leave for 10 minutes.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured work surface for 8 minutes, until elastic. The dough will remain slightly sticky. Shape into a ball, place in an oiled bowl, and turn to coat. Cover and leave for 1 hour, until doubled.
  3. Lightly oil a baking sheet. Punch down the dough and knead. Divide into 16 balls and place on the sheet. Cover with oiled plastic wrap and leave for 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Brush the egg over the rolls and sprinkle with oats. Bake for 15-20 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.
