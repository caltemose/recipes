Gorgonzola Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 unit(s) fennel bulb(s)
  • 2 unit(s) carrot(s)
  • 5 unit(s) celery
  • 1 unit(s) granny smith apple(s)
  • 1 clove(s) garlic finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  • 150 gram(s) gorgonzola
  • 1 tablespoon(s) parsley finely chopped

Directions

    Notes

    Discard the outer leaves of the fennel and cut the bulbs in half. Wash and cut into 1/8 inch thick slices. Trim the carrots, scrape and cut into julienne strips. Trim the celery, removing any damaged or touch outer leaves and cut the stalks in slices like the fennel. Wash the apple and cut in thin wedges. Put the garlic, apple and vegetables in a salad bowl and season with salt, pepper and 2 tablespoons of oil. Dice the gorgonzola and put it in a heavy-bottomed saucepan with 1 tablespoon of oil. Melt slowly over low heat, stirring continuously. When it is barely lukewarm and has become creamy, pour it over the vegetables. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
