Spinach Egg Ham Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 6 unit(s) egg(s)
  • 0.25 cup(s) coconut flour
  • 1 teaspoon(s) fish sauce or salt to taste
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup(s) ham diced
  • 10 ounce(s) spinach or whatever greens
  • 2 unit(s) scallions
  • 1 tablespoon(s) cilantro minced
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) baking soda
  • 1 to taste black pepper

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, coconut flour, fish sauce, and apple cider vinegar until smooth.
  2. <div>Mix in the ham, spinach, scallions, cilantro, and baking soda, and some freshly ground black pepper.</div>
  3. Melt ghee/oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Drop the batter into the pan to form pancakes about 1/2 inch thick.
  5. Fry 2 minutes then flip and cook 1-2 minutes more. Pancakes are cooked when the center bounces back when you press down on them with your finger.
