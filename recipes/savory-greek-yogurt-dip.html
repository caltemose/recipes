Savory Greek Yogurt Dip

Alton Brown

Ingredients

  • 1 quart(s) greek yogurt plain
  • 1 tablespoon(s) parsley flat leaf, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon(s) mint chopped
  • 1 teaspoon(s) ground cumin ground
  • 1 teaspoon(s) lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon(s) black pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients. Transfer to an airtight container, cover and refrigerate for up to 10 days.
