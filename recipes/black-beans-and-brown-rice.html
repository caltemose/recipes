Black Beans and Brown Rice

Everyday Cook

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup(s) brown rice
  • 0.25 cup(s) olive oil
  • 1.5 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 4 clove(s) garlic smashed
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) chili pepper flakes
  • 1 unit(s) onion(s) chopped
  • 15 ounce(s) black beans 1 can
  • 1 teaspoon(s) oregano
  • 0.25 teaspoon(s) black pepper
  • 1 unit(s) avocado diced
  • 1

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375F.
  2. Spread rice into an 8-inch square glass baking dish. Stir in 1 TBS olive oil and 1 ts salt.
  3. Bring 2.5 cups water to boil and pour over the rice. Cover tightly with aluminum foil and back for 1 hour.
  4. Place a 12-inch saut√© pan over medium heat. Add remaining 3 TBS olive oil and garlic and pepper flakes. Cook slightly, add onion and 1/2 ts salt and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the onion starts to color.
  5. Add black beans, oregano and black pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, for 6-8 minutes.
  6. Remove the cover from the rice and fluff with a fork.
  7. Serve the beans over the rice and garnish with avocado.
