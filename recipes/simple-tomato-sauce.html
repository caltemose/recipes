Simple Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 clove(s) garlic finely chopped
  • 4 olive oil
  • 2 basil fresh leaves, torn
  • 28 ounce(s) canned tomatoes 1 large can

Directions

    Notes

    Put the garlic and oil in a large skillet and sauté over medium heat until the garlic is pale gold. Add the tomatoes and basil. Season with salt and pepper and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the oil begins to separate from the tomato. Fresh tomatoes can be used if in they are season. Remove the skins and chop before using. Variations: - Sauté 1 small onion, 1 carrot, 1 stalk celery, 1 tablespoon parsley, all finely chopped, with the garlic. - Add a bay leaf, crushed chillies, anchovy fillet(s) or capers to the sauce when you add the tomatoes.
